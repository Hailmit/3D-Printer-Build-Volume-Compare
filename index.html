How to use

Scale: 1 unit = 1 mm. Z is vertical; boxes rest on the build plate; all printers share the same world space.

Axes: X red, Y green, Z blue.

Controls: Scroll or right-drag to zoom/rotate. Checkboxes toggle visibility. Volumes shown in liters (1 mm³ = 1e-6 L).
